Jacksonville Downtown Artwalk

The First Wednesday of Every Month

Rain or Shine

Presented by Downtown Vision

Upcoming Art Walk

Wednesday Aug 7, 2013

Theme: A Taste of the Arts

Art Walk Map

Produced by Downtown Vision, Inc. and held each month from 5 p.m. to 9 p.m.—rain or shine—the monthly First Wednesday Art Walk spans more than 15 blocks that includes more than 40 galleries, museums, cultural venues, restaurants, bars and businesses, plus dozens of artists in Hemming Plaza, street performers and live music.

Want to get in touch? Reach out to Art Walk manager Liz Grebe at liz@downtownjacksonville.org or call 904-634-0303, ext. 225.

Don’t Miss Venues

Bon Appétit! A Taste of the Arts features the art of food. Little chefs decorate chef hats in Hemming Plaza while all ages visit the venues for great tastings and presentations.

Featured Venues

  • Headquarters
    @ Hemming Plaza

    Enjoy live music by Slickwater. Decorate your own chef hat and enjoy treats from The Brick Coffee House. Valet your bike along Laura Street with Bike Jax.

  • Southlight Gallery

    Presents A Visual Feast featuring fine art by local artists and live music with Kara Lynn Frazier from 7-9 p.m.

  • Florida Theatre

    Tour FT at Art Walk! Take a free tour at 5:30, 6:30 or 7:30 p.m. to learn more about the iconic Florida Theatre.

  • Midtown Deli + Cafe

    Enjoy the Dark Arts Bazaar with unconventional creations, indulge in homemade Blood Orange Cupcake and food, and drink specials.

  • Yates Parking Garage Mural Dedication

    Join the Cultural Council of Greater Jacksonville and the Mayor’s Administration at the First Presbyterian Church parking lot from 6-7 p.m. for the dedication ceremony of the murals created by Sean Mahan and Milagros Art Collective. Enjoy live music by Alex Hayward & Friends.

  • juice - a unity plaza gallery

    Group Visual Artists Showcase hosts The Art of Deliciousness with an array of culinary presentations and live jazz music.

  • Mad Men Marketing

    Enjoy Sweet Pete’s candy and the Old City Oil Dripper’s showcase of old restored Volkswagen cars as art. Enjoy watching a VW Beetle turn into a piece of art work with a live demonstration.

  • Aurora App

    Download the Aurora App to learn about secret shows during Art Walk.
